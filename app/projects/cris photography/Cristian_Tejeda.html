Cristian Tejeda

Description

Cristian Tejeda is a young introvert photographer from the Dominican Republic. He began photography out of the need to express what he wanted to say to other people, so that people like him they don't feel alone and have someone to count on. His goal is to capture the small details that bring out the simplicity and beauty of life.
